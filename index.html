Overview

Exiled Architect is a local pre-builder for creating Conan Exiles base plans with square and triangle foundations. Build, test, save, export, and share your layouts easily.
snap précisofflineJSON

Dimensions

Square : side length 1. Triangle : equilateral, side length 1.

Floors / Layers

Create one layer per floor, then switch back any time to edit the lower levels.

Advanced tools

Selection mode: click and drag to select several pieces, then press Delete to remove the block. Symmetry mode automatically places a mirrored copy around the plan’s vertical axis.

Tips

Save your plans often, export JSON files to share your builds, and use “Force place” only when a valid piece is blocked in a rare edge case.
